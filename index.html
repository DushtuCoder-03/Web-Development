My top 3 movies of all-time

Your Name

It has great animations and art styles, also has a very satisfying storyline.

Grave of The Fireflies

It shows the situation of World War II in Japan where two orphan siblings tried their best to survive and hoped for a peaceful world where they can laugh and live happily with their parents and family again.

Spirited Away

It's a masterpiece made by Studio Ghibli Japan. You must watch it also, I'm sure you'll enjoy it.
